## Strategies for Booking Flights

Timing is crucial when purchasing international airfare. You should aim to book your tickets several months in advance. Last-minute fares are rarely cheaper, especially for long-haul destinations. 

Flexibility is another vital factor in finding the best deals. If you can alter your travel dates by a few days, you might discover significant savings. Mid-week flights are often less expensive than those departing on weekends.

You should also consider alternative routes. Direct flights are convenient, but indirect flights with short layovers can sometimes offer substantial discounts. You must weigh the cost savings against the additional travel time to determine the best option for your needs.
