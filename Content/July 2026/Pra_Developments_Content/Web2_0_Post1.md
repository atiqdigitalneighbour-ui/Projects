## Understanding the Turnkey Solution

One of the most popular options for new buyers and investors is a turnkey property solution. A turnkey home means the property is completely finished and ready for you to move into on the day of handover. You do not need to worry about organising external contractors for painting, landscaping, or installing flooring after the builder finishes their portion of the work. Everything is handled by the developer from start to finish.

This is an excellent choice for individuals who want a streamlined building experience. By rolling all costs and tasks into a single contract, buyers achieve greater financial certainty. ## Budgeting for Your New Home

Establishing a realistic budget is a fundamental step that must happen before you sign any contracts. It is important to account for all costs associated with the build, not just the base price of the house. This includes site works, utility connections, and potential upgrades to fixtures and fittings. A turnkey contract often provides the most accurate reflection of the final cost, as it includes the finishing touches that many traditional contracts leave out.

Speak with a financial advisor or mortgage broker early in the process to understand your borrowing capacity. This ensures you target properties and designs that align with your financial situation, preventing unnecessary stress later in the project.
